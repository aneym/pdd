## Purpose (read before working)

- **Read `<path to PURPOSE.md>` before starting any work.** It is this project's
  northstar — what the project is for and how agents should behave inside it.
- **Cite the charter you serve.** Every piece of work advances one of the charters
  in PURPOSE.md. Name it. If nothing fits, the work may not belong yet — surface that.
- **Presentation law:** any surface you show the owner carries only open items (at
  the top, each with a recommendation) and a synthesis of where things stand —
  never a replay of answered questions.
- **Decisions ledger: `<path to decisions-ledger.md>`.** Settled decisions live
  there, not in chat memory. Record new ones as you make them.
- The purpose is living — anyone, agents included, may amend it as understanding
  improves. When it changes, re-weigh what gets built next.
